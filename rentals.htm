Reserve Your Bike

Josephs Bicycles has several different bikes for you to rent either daily or weekly. Daily rentals will be available durning buisnees hours and will be returned same day. Weekly rentals must be returned prior to 9 a.m. on the final day. If you decide to purchase the a bike we will take the daily rental fee off the price. Roscoe 6 is the perfect gatebay bike. This mountain bike is ideal for new riders or someone who is looking to cruz trails. Roscoe 7 is great for new riders and experienced ones too. The bike is extremely stable and will give every rider a big smile.

Domane AL 2 is perfect for new riders looking to get into this fantastic sport. It is light and easy to accessorize. Domane AL 2 Disc is a next level experience. It builds upon everything that is great about the Domane AL 2 It is lightweight, stable, and has disc breaks. You will look forward to your next ride.

As always safety is our number one priority. When you rent a bike you will need to have a helmet. You can either rent one from us for a fee of $5 a day or you can bring your own.
